a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away...
It is a period of galactic civil war. Email clients are fractured and render HTML in over a dozen different ways. Developers rebel against Imperial tyrany and fight to bring order and a consistent user experience to the inbox.
Determined forces
break through Imperial blockades and table boundaries with illusions of color and shape.

Using the power of nested tables and conditional code...

...these freedom fighters bring solid responsive design to users...

...from the masses in the Core Worlds to the edge cases in the Outer Rim.
But

the
Dark Side
 
still threatens to choke creativity with iron-fisted email preprocessors that intercept crucial segments of code and merciless word-processing rendering engines that ignore common styles like max-width.
